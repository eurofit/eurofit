# Product Marketing Context

_Last updated: 2026-06-17_

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** Eurofit is Kenya's biggest supplier of sports nutrition, supplements, and vitamins — serving retail customers, gyms, and wholesale buyers nationwide.

**What it does:** Eurofit imports and supplies 100% authentic European sports nutrition, supplements, and vitamins at the largest scale in Kenya. Customers shop online with countrywide delivery or visit the flagship Nairobi store, and order at both retail and wholesale prices. From a single tub of protein to a gym's full restock, Eurofit is the country's go-to source.

**Product category:** Sports nutrition & dietary supplements retailer/wholesaler (e-commerce + physical store).

**Product type:** E-commerce store (Payload CMS + Next.js) with an attached brick-and-mortar retail/wholesale operation.

**Business model:** Direct B2C retail + B2B wholesale/distribution. Online checkout (Paystack) plus WhatsApp price-inquiry for bulk and brand-specific orders. Payments: M-Pesa, Cash, Bank Transfer (KES).

## Target Audience

**Target companies:** Gyms, fitness studios, spas, supplement resellers, and distributors across Kenya and East Africa.

**Decision-makers:** Gym owners/managers, retail shop buyers, distributors — plus individual fitness consumers buying for themselves.

**Primary use case:** Sourcing genuine, well-priced sports nutrition and supplements reliably — without fear of counterfeits or stockouts.

**Jobs to be done:**

- **Use cases:**

- Gym owner places a recurring bulk wholesale order to stock a pro shop.
- Reseller/distributor partners with Eurofit to supply their own region.
- Customer outside Nairobi orders online for nationwide courier delivery.

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** Kenya's supplement market is flooded with counterfeit and grey-market products, unreliable shops, and inconsistent pricing — buyers can't tell genuine from fake or find a supplier who never runs out.

**Why alternatives fall short:** Small shops carry limited, often unverified stock; informal sellers can't guarantee authenticity; importing yourself is slow and risky.

- **What it costs them:** Wasted money on fakes, health risk, lost gym revenue from stockouts, time chasing unreliable suppliers.

**Emotional tension:** "Is this real or counterfeit?" — anxiety about being scammed, and frustration with suppliers who disappoint.

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct:** Local supplement shops & online sellers — fall short on authenticity guarantees, catalogue breadth, and wholesale scale.

**Secondary:** Personal importing / overseas online orders — fall short on speed, shipping cost, customs risk, and no local support.

**Indirect:** Pharmacies & general fitness stores — fall short on specialist range, expertise, and wholesale pricing.

## Differentiation

**Key differentiators:**

- Both **retail and wholesale** under one roof.

**Why that's better:** Guaranteed genuine products, prices that work for individuals and businesses, and a supplier big enough to never leave you out of stock.

**Why customers choose us:** Trust (authenticity), scale (always in stock), reach (delivers everywhere), and price (retail + wholesale).
