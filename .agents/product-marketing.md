# Product Marketing Context

_Last updated: 2026-06-06_

## Product Overview

**One-liner:** Kenya's biggest supplier of authentic European sports nutrition, supplements, and vitamins — for both retail and wholesale.

**What it does:** EuroFit (Eurofit Health & Beauty LTD) sources and distributes 100% authentic sports nutrition products, supplements, and vitamins from authorized European manufacturers. It sells directly to fitness enthusiasts and athletes (retail), and supplies gyms, fitness shops, and distributors at wholesale prices. Orders can be placed online, via WhatsApp, or in-store at their Nairobi location.

**Product category:** Sports nutrition & supplement supplier / e-commerce store

**Product type:** B2B + B2C e-commerce marketplace with physical presence

**Business model:** Direct product sales (retail KES pricing) + wholesale partnerships (gyms, retailers, distributors). Payments via M-Pesa, bank transfer, and cash. Nationwide delivery across Kenya and East Africa.

## Problems & Pain Points

**Core problem:** The Kenyan supplements market is flooded with counterfeit and sub-standard products. Buyers can't easily verify authenticity, and genuine products are scattered across multiple unreliable vendors.

**Why alternatives fall short:**

- Local pharmacies carry limited fitness-specific brands
- Random online sellers offer no authenticity guarantees
- Importing directly from Europe is expensive, slow, and complex for individuals
- No single Kenyan supplier has covered both retail and wholesale at scale

**What it costs them:**

- Money wasted on fake or ineffective products
- Health risks from counterfeit supplements
- Time spent hunting across multiple vendors
- Missed gains/results from inconsistent supply

**Emotional tension:** Frustration and distrust — "Am I actually buying what it says on the label?"

---

## Competitive Landscape

**Direct:** Other Kenyan supplement importers/resellers — fall short because they can't verify European sourcing, have limited brand range, or don't serve wholesale

**Secondary:** Local pharmacies — fall short because they stock minimal sports nutrition and serve retail only, no wholesale

**Indirect:** Importing directly from Europe — falls short because it's expensive, slow, complex customs clearance, and inaccessible for most individuals

## Switching Dynamics

**Push:** Distrust of current supplier after receiving suspected counterfeit products; inconsistent stock availability; no wholesale option.

**Pull:** Guarantee of 100% authentic European products; one-stop shop for 100+ brands; nationwide delivery; WhatsApp ordering convenience.

**Habit:** Comfort with existing suppliers ("I've always bought from X"); loyalty to a local gym's recommendation.

**Anxiety:** "What if delivery takes too long?" / "What if minimum wholesale order is too high?" / "Is the online store trustworthy?"
